# Architecture and Policy Improvements

To strengthen the overall security posture of the system, several architectural and configuration improvements were proposed.

## Principle of Least Privilege
Users are granted only the minimum permissions required to perform their tasks.

## Centralized Authorization
Access control checks are enforced at the server level to ensure consistent authorization decisions.

## Secure Configuration
Security-focused configurations such as secure cookies, session timeouts, and proper logout mechanisms were implemented.

## Defense-in-Depth
Multiple layers of protection were introduced to reduce the chances of successful exploitation.
